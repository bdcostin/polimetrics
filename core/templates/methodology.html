Caveats

Like all opinion polls, our data isn't perfect. The reality is that not all voters use Twitter. More specifically, the age groups that make up the United States population are not portionate to the age groups that use Twitter. Our solution is to weight the results of our sentiment analysis. In an effort to provide the most insight to our users, we have made weighted and unweighted analysis available on the application.
